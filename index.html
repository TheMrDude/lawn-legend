🌿 Welcome to Lawn Legend!

Build a landscaping empire from nothing. Start with a push mower, grow into a multi-truck fleet operation.

🎮 How to Play

Click a job card in the sidebar to start working. If you have multiple trucks, pick which truck to send!

Switch neighborhoods using the green buttons above the job list. Each area has different jobs — you can work multiple neighborhoods per day!

Drag or scroll the canvas to look around the neighborhood.

🚛 Multi-Truck Dispatch

This is the key to building an empire! Each truck you own is an independent crew. When you click a job:

• 1 truck: It auto-dispatches to the job.

• 2+ trucks: Pick which truck to send. Each truck works its job simultaneously!

• Truck 1 can mow on Maple St while Truck 2 trims hedges on Oak Ave — at the same time.

• Switch neighborhoods freely to dispatch trucks to different streets.

• The "Active Crews" bar shows all trucks currently working across all neighborhoods.

⏰ Day Timer

Each day has 8 work hours (upgradeable). Every job costs time — deducted when work completes. The clock is shared across all trucks and neighborhoods. When you're out of time, end the day.

🚛 Building Your Fleet

Buy trucks in the Crew tab. Each truck holds up to 5-8 crew. More trucks = more simultaneous jobs = more money per day!

Truck 1 (Used Pickup) = 5 crew cap. Truck 2 (Work Truck) = +5. Truck 3 (Box Truck) = +6. Truck 4 (Fleet Van) = +8.

📚 Training Crew

Train crew to level up (1 day downtime). Higher levels = faster work (+5%/lvl), better pay (+8%/lvl), and happier customers.

🏘️ Neighborhoods

Unlock new areas by earning reputation stars. Higher-tier areas pay more but expect more. Invest in Area Upgrades for permanent bonuses.

📝 Contracts & Bids

Win bids against rival companies for recurring contract income. Better signage = more bid opportunities.

🎯 Goals & Rankings

Complete weekly goals for bonus rewards. Climb the local rankings by growing your company valuation above rival companies.

💡 Tips

• Chain jobs fast for combo multipliers
• Complete all jobs in a day for Perfect Day bonus
• Keep equipment maintained — worn tools slow you down
• Buy a second truck ASAP — it doubles your earning power
• Dispatch trucks to different neighborhoods for max coverage
